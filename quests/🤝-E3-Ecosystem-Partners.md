# 🤝 E3 — Ecosystem / Experts / Partners

**Sphere:** E | **Quest Line:** Building the Network That Makes Your Venture Possible

> *"You don't need to know everything. You need to know who knows."*

---

## 🎯 Quest Overview

| | |
|---|---|
| **Sphere** | E — Entrepreneurship |
| **Core question** | Who already has what you need, and how do you connect with them? |
| **Best for** | People who enjoy connecting others and investing in relationships long-term |
| **Good first output** | Ecosystem map or outreach draft |

---

## 📖 Theory Bite

| Concept | What it means |
|---------|---------------|
| Stakeholder mapping | Who matters for your success? (investors, advisors, partners, regulators) |
| Social capital | Relationships are assets — invest before you need them |
| Strategic partnerships | Who already has the users/data/distribution you need? |
| Expert leverage | You don't need to know everything — you need to know who knows |

**Daily reality:** Networking, writing outreach messages, attending events, and maintaining relationships with no immediate payoff.

### Quest E3.2 — Outreach Draft
**⏱ Time:** ~20 min | **Difficulty:** ⭐⭐ Intermediate

**Goal:** Write a cold outreach message under 100 words.

**Rules:**
- Make it about **them**, not you
- One clear ask
- Show you've done your homework

<details>
<summary>💡 Outreach template</summary>

> Hi [Name],
> 
> I noticed [specific thing they did/wrote]. That resonates because [1-sentence connection to your work].
> 
> I'm working on [1-sentence description] and would value [specific ask: 15 min call / feedback on one thing / introduction to ___].
> 
> No pressure if timing doesn't work.
> 
> [Your name]

</details>
