### Quest E2.2 — Interview Script
**⏱ Time:** ~30 min | **Difficulty:** ⭐⭐ Intermediate

**Goal:** Write 5 customer interview questions that actually work.

**Rules:**
- No "would you use this?" → people lie to be nice
- No "do you think this is a good idea?" → opinions ≠ behavior
- Only "tell me about the last time you..." → reveals real behavior

<details>
<summary>💡 Good vs. Bad interview questions</summary>

| Bad | Why | Good instead |
|-----|-----|-------------|
| "Would you use an app that tracks habits?" | People say yes to hypotheticals | "How do you currently track your habits?" |
| "Do you think this is a good idea?" | Opinions ≠ behavior | "When did you last try to solve this problem?" |
| "How much would you pay?" | Stated preferences ≠ revealed preferences | "What have you already spent money on for this?" |

</details>

### Quest E2.3 — GTM Map
**⏱ Time:** ~30 min | **Difficulty:** ⭐⭐ Intermediate

**Goal:** Map how users will discover your product.

```
GTM MAP
├── 👥 Audience: Who are they? (1 sentence)
├── 🎯 Need: What do they want?
├── 💬 Message: What do you say to them?
├── 📢 First channel: Where do you reach them?
└── 📏 Success metric: How do you know it worked?
```

### Quest E2.4 — Landing Page Test
**⏱ Time:** ~45 min | **Difficulty:** ⭐⭐ Intermediate

**Goal:** Write copy for a fake landing page. No product exists yet — can you still get signups?

**Steps:**
1. Write:
   - **Headline** (6-10 words, benefit-focused)
   - **Subheadline** (1 sentence expanding the headline)
   - **3 bullet benefits**
   - **One proof point** (testimonial, stat, or demo)
   - **CTA** (what should they do?)

<details>
<summary>💡 Headline formulas that work</summary>

- "Do [outcome] without [pain point]"
- "The [category] for [specific audience]"  
- "Stop [pain]. Start [benefit]."
- "[Number] ways to [outcome] in [timeframe]"

</details>
